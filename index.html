People in Need

For people in need, we created a central hub and the perfect place to find all of the essential resources they want or need. Knowing where to look and where to turn is one of the hardest battles these people face. CivicServAnt effortlessly brings volunteers and resources to those that need it the most. With one tap of the finger or one click of the mouse, all of the sudden the world is at your fingertips. No hassle, no stress, and no running in circles. Day or night, there is no better time to seek help then the present.

Volunteers

For volunteers, creating a first ever “gamified” experience in the world of volunteering was a top priority. You thought that volunteering was fun before? Wait until you see CivicServAnt. We incorporated a reward-based volunteering experience, that puts an extra incentive behind the work of those giving their time and effort. Volunteering has never been this exciting! Tracking volunteer experience allows users to quantify their work and easily see what they have accomplished. No guess work is involved because all of your history is forever at your fingertips. By including a “quick button” for helping people, we have created a one-step way to efficiently provide a helping hand, whether you’re walking down the street with friends, or at an organized event.

Non-profits

For Nonprofit organizations, we provide a way to connect a database of information that was once difficult to track down. We will allow easy setup for events in this network, and create an environment of collaboration between companies and non profits. Easier volunteer tracking makes getting involved in the community easier than ever before. We remove the hassle by providing a go-to center for the Jacksonville community. With one simple place to find events throughout the community, people will have no problem finding the perfect event for them. We take everything into consideration, from the time of year, the type of event, and much more.

Companies

For companies, connecting a database of information makes for a pleasant experience. With easier employee-volunteer time tracking and management tools, there is no excuse to not get involved. Companies are always looking to get involved in their community, but it is not always an easy process. So why not let us do all the hard work, and make the end result seamless and efficient? We provide the missing link between companies and their community.
